I am a PhD student at the University of Stavanger in the SFI DigiWells advised by Dr. Dan Sui, Dr. Benoit Daireaux and Dr. Sergey Alyaev.

My work involves developing reinforcement learning systems for automated drilling optimization. I approach this as (1) learning hierarchical policies for autonomous drilling operations and (2) bridging simulation-based planning with real-time operational decision-making. A key piece of this is managing uncertainties in complex subsurface operations.

Previously, I worked as a Data Scientist at Eavor Technologies where I developed signal processing algorithms for magnetic ranging and wellbore positioning.
